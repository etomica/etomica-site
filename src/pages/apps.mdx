
# Apps

Web applications that perform calculations of interest in molecular modeling. These run in your browser.

- [LJ EOS](pathname:///apps/LJ_EOS) Equation of state for the Lennard-Jones model, providing very accurate and precise values for a variety of thermodynamic properties. Explore the effect of potential truncation and system size, or compare various equations of state from the literature. 
- [Virial Coefficients](pathname:///apps/virial)
- [Simulation](pathname:///apps/simulation)
