The Lennard-Jones solid and liquid phases are in equilibrium starting at the vapor-liquid-FCC triple point and extending to the soft-sphere limit at infinite temperature and density. This page will trace the whole curve from triple point to the soft-sphere limit. Because the very-high temperature data will throw off the scale of most coexistence plots, maximum values density and temperature can be set. Alternatively, you can plot v2 = ρ-2 and Y = (4/T)ρ-4, which remain finite over the whole curve.
